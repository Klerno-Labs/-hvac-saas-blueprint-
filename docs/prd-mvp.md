# MVP PRD

## Product
AI-assisted residential HVAC quote-to-payment SaaS.

## Core user
Owner/operator or office manager at a small residential HVAC business with 1–5 technicians.

## Core problem
Shops lose time and delay cash collection because quotes, proof-of-work, invoices, and payment collection are fragmented.

## Core promise
Get paid faster on every HVAC job.

## Activation event
First payment collected on a live invoice.

## Primary success metrics
- first-payment activation within 14 days
- time to first payment
- weekly active paying organizations
- support time per paying account

## In-scope MVP capabilities
- account creation and organization onboarding
- customer and job creation
- AI-assisted estimate drafting
- job completion and proof-of-work upload
- invoice creation and sending
- payment collection via Stripe Connect
- unpaid invoice queue
- simple cash dashboard

## Out-of-scope MVP capabilities
- inventory
- financing
- payroll
- commercial workflows
- native mobile app
- accounting sync beyond future roadmap

## Main user story
As an HVAC owner, I want to move from customer/job setup to invoice payment inside one simple workflow so I can collect money faster with less admin work.
