# Validation Scorecard

## Outreach thresholds
- Reply rate
  - Pass: 6%+
  - Warning: 3%–5.9%
  - Fail: under 3%
- Trial-start rate from targeted sends
  - Pass: 2%+
  - Warning: 0.8%–1.9%
  - Fail: under 0.8%
- Qualified positive reply rate
  - Pass: 3%+
  - Warning: 1%–2.9%
  - Fail: under 1%

## Activation thresholds
- First payment within 14 days
  - Pass: 40%+
  - Warning: 20%–39%
  - Fail: under 20%

## Retention thresholds
- Weekly active paying accounts before collections automation
  - Preferred: 25+
  - Minimum: 15–20
  - Too early: under 15

## Support thresholds
- Founder/support time per new paying account in month one
  - Good: under 45 minutes
  - Warning: 45–90 minutes
  - Bad: over 90 minutes

## Headline change trigger
If the dominant repeated objection is: “getting paid faster is not my urgent problem,” change the homepage headline and test a new positioning angle.

## Pivot rule
If reply rate, trial-start rate, and first-payment activation all land in fail territory after a serious test cycle, pivot message, segment, or wedge before building more.
