Goal: finalize the operating thesis for a self-serve residential HVAC SaaS focused on faster payment collection.
Output:
1. ICP and excluded customer segments.
2. Core value proposition and anti-positioning.
3. Exact activation metric, retention metric, and pivot thresholds.
4. MVP feature list and explicit non-goals.
5. Outreach message, homepage copy, and trust proof requirements.
Constraints:
- Must target owner-led residential HVAC shops with 1–5 techs.
- Must prioritize self-serve and async acquisition.
- Must avoid enterprise/commercial complexity.
- Must define numeric pass/warning/fail thresholds.
Do not generate code. Produce a reviewable plan only.
